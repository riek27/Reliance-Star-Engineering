Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and how we can help bring your project to life.

Where are you located in South Sudan?

We are headquartered in Gumbo – Sherikat, Juba, South Sudan (Postal Code: 81111). From our Juba office, we serve clients across all major regions of South Sudan, providing engineering and consultancy services nationwide.

How do I get a quote for my engineering project?

Simply reach out via our contact form, call us at +211 928 800 108, or email info@reliancestar.com. Our team will schedule a consultation to understand your needs, conduct a site assessment if required, and provide a detailed, transparent quotation tailored to your project.

What types of projects do you specialize in?

We specialize in architectural design, structural engineering, engineering consultancy, general construction services, and building maintenance. Our team handles residential, commercial, institutional, and industrial projects of varying scales across South Sudan.

What makes Reliance Star different from other firms?

We combine high-quality engineering solutions with professional, reliable consultancy. Our skilled and qualified engineering team is committed to timely project delivery and a client-focused approach. We value professionalism, continuous learning, and team development to ensure the highest standards on every project.
